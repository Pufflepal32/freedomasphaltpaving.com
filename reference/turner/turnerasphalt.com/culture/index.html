Culture

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled quality of work and build better communities by promoting an inclusive environment where every employee is valued, safe, respected, and empowered to contribute their best.

Our Values

At Turner Asphalt, we practice what we preach. Whether we’re performing asphalt repairs or scheduling a service over the phone, we’re guided by the same key principles on which we were founded. After all, actions speak louder than words.

We Are Committed

We focus on what matters most. We prioritize investing in our people, tools, and technology to achieve unprecedented results.

We Are Genuine

Authenticity and honesty are at the core of all our interactions. We see the beauty in hard work and hold a deep and genuine appreciation for every team member and their contributions on the job and in their communities.

We Are Exceptional

We put our trust in our partners and our people. We exceed expectations by delivering exceptional paving solutions that not only transform spaces but also reflect the dedication and skill of our team.

We’re Committed To…

Keeping You Safe

We believe in doing the job the right way. That means providing the tools, training, and support our employees need to deliver services safely and efficiently from start to finish. This helps us minimize delays and reduce the risk of accidents and injuries on the job.

Putting People First

Turner Asphalt is built on a diverse workforce of hardworking paving professionals. Every person we employ, partner with, and serve has the potential to improve the lives of people in their communities. That’s why we invest our time and resources into hiring top candidates and supporting them along the way.

Everyone Has a Place at Turner Asphalt

We cultivate an environment that embraces diversity, fosters inclusion, and nurtures a sense of belonging for all employees.

inclusion & Belonging

Transparency

Ethical Conduct

Uphold the highest ethical standards in all business dealings, maintaining integrity and honesty.

Risk Management

Implement robust risk management strategies to safeguard against potential ESG-related challenges and uncertainties.

Safety

Committed to Keeping You Safe

Employee health and safety are top priorities at Turner Asphalt. That’s why we take measures to maintain an accident and injury-free work environment. From tools to training and programs, our safety strategies start with our management team and extend to every employee.

Protecting Our People Through…

Safety Training

We provide all employees with comprehensive safety training that’s tailored to their job duties. This training includes lessons on hazard recognition, equipment operation, and emergency response procedures.

Job Site Inspections

We perform routine inspections of our job sites to identify potential hazards and ensure that all safety protocols are being followed.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

We provide our work crews with the appropriate PPE for their job duties. This includes hard hats, safety glasses, gloves, high visibility vests, and respiratory protection as needed.

Safety Committees

Each job site has a designated safety committee that’s dedicated to promoting safety awareness and encouraging employees to uphold all safety standards. These committees meet regularly throughout the year to review safety performance, identify potential hazards, and develop strategies for accident prevention.

Incident Reporting and Investigation

We require immediate reporting for all workplace incidents, including near-misses. We also conduct thorough investigations of all incidents to develop solutions that will prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future.

Continuous Improvement

We continuously evaluate our safety program to identify areas for improvement. We welcome feedback from our employees and customers to help us identify areas where we can enhance our safety performance.
