Parking Lot Paving & Routine Maintenance

Your parking lot contributes significantly to your property’s value and safety. You need a paving contractor committed to delivering expert preventative and routine maintenance services to keep it looking and functioning at its best. 

Turner Asphalt aims to prevent, restore, and enhance your lot’s appearance, navigation, and safety for employees and customers who visit with our premier parking lot paving and maintenance services: 

  • Sealcoating: We add a protective layer on top of the existing pavement. This layer is a preventative measure, protecting your lot from damage and wear and tear, such as cracks, potholes, or chemical and water infiltration. Sealcoating effectively enhances your surface’s lifespan. 
  • Asphalt Striping: Proper and visible pavement markings are crucial to ensure pedestrian safety and traffic flow. Your existing markings and striping naturally wear and fade over time, so give your lot a refresh and improve safety with our custom asphalt striping services.
  • Safety Features: Turner Asphalt offers additional safety features, like textured or slip-resistant surfaces, speed bumps, and curb ramps to enhance traffic flow and safety on your property.

ADA Compliant Parking Lots

Your property is only entirely safe when it is accessible to all. Turner Asphalt contractors are well-versed in the legal ADA requirements and will assist you in ensuring your property remains in compliance and accessible. 

We will support you in making sure your lot is fully ADA-compliant with these steps:  

  1. Review & understand the ADA regulations.
  2. Inspect the site for ADA compliance and address any issues.
  3. Implement any regulatory steps for ADA additions (i.e., pavement markings, safety features, or signage).

Concrete Curbing, Sidewalks & Flatwork

Concrete is one of the most durable paving materials on the market. It is ideal for surfaces that face heavy loads or high traffic, making it perfect for many commercial properties. 

As Atlanta’s most trusted concrete contractor, our paving specialists deliver top-tier concrete surface maintenance and repairs for: 

  • Sidewalks
  • Curbs & Gutters 
  • Dumpster Pads & Loading Docks 
  • Stairs & Breezeways

Asphalt Repairs, Milling, & Full Depth Reclamation

If your existing pavement shows significant signs of wear and tear, it means your property is thriving with increased customer traffic, and we want to help you keep it that way. 

Let us maintain and enhance the integrity of your lot with asphalt repairs, milling, and full-depth reclamation services to get its shine and smoothness back. 

  • Patching & Pothole Repair: Addresses damaged areas quickly to restore safety and prevent further wear. 
  • Crack Filling: Seals cracks to prevent water intrusion and extend pavement lifespan. 
  • Repaving: Lays fresh asphalt over worn surfaces for a smooth, durable finish.
  • Milling & Reclamation: Removes and recycles old asphalt to rebuild a stronger foundation.
