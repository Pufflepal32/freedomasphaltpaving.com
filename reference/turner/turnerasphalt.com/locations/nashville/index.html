Milling and Reclamation

In Nashville’s dynamic urban landscape, our cost-effective milling and reclamation services revitalize paved surfaces while prioritizing environmental sustainability. Milling is ideal for surfaces with strong underlying sub-grades showing visible wear. This process allows us to grind down and remove the top layer of asphalt, and install new asphalt over top. For projects where both surface and subgrade are compromised, reclamation might be the best approach. This “green” method recycles existing materials, reducing the need for extensive material transport and minimizing environmental impact. Our Nashville commercial paving experts provide these services for:
  • Road resurfacing preparation
  • Parking lots
  • Full or partial depth patching
  • Perimeter transitions to integrate new pavement with old
Turner Asphalt’s team in Nashville is equipped with state-of-the-art milling machinery and the expertise to guide you toward the most efficient and cost-effective solution for your project.

SOIL STABILIZATION

In Tennessee’s diverse soil conditions, stabilization is a crucial technique for creating robust foundations, or sub-grades, for paving projects. This method not only potentially reduces costs but also aligns with eco-friendly practices by minimizing soil displacement and material transportation. Turner Asphalt employs various stabilization techniques tailored to Nashville’s unique geological profile, ensuring optimal results for each project. Whether you’re developing a new commercial site or upgrading existing infrastructure, our soil stabilization services provide a sustainable foundation for your paving needs.

CONCRETE & CURBING

Concrete is beneficial for use in areas that require more strength. In these cases, it can be an ideal substitute for asphalt. Generally speaking, concrete is best suited for the following projects:

Driveways

To make driveways longer-lasting and more durable, we use concrete.

Sidewalks

In a high-traffic area such as sidewalks, a concrete base can help last longer with less upkeep and repairs needed!

Curbs & Gutters

Concrete curbs and gutters create a solid border for pavers to lay down the road surface.

Dumpster Pads & Loading Docks

Concrete is a strong material that will give these high-traffic and high-impact areas extra strength.

Stairs & Breezeways

With a lot of foot traffic from people passing through, stairs and breezeways can ensure these areas are long-lasting and enduring.
