Pavement Milling and Full Depth Reclamation

Asphalt milling and full depth reclamation are two cost-effective and environmentally friendly tools we have at our disposal to help make your pavement more stable and give it a fresh look.

With pavement milling, if the sub-grade is still strong and intact— even though the asphalt is looking a little worse-for-wear— we can mill it to look new again. If the base has been compromised, pavement reclamation might be the better process.

If you’re unsure of what will work best for you, our team of paving contractors in Wilmington can help!

We provide this service for:

  • Road resurfacing preparation
  • Parking lots
  • Full or partial depth patching
  • Perimeter transitions to integrate new pavement with old
Here at Turner Asphalt, we have a fleet of high-quality milling machines ready to be leased by the day and trained staff ready to operate them!

CONCRETE & CURBING

When your paving area requires more strength than asphalt, concrete can be an ideal solution. Concrete and curbing can work hand-in-hand to create a strong surface that will last. We suggest concrete in certain projects, including:

Driveways

Home and business owners who want to pave it and then forget it, choose high-quality concrete for their driveways.

Sidewalks

With a concrete base, the sidewalk will last for many, many years without as much upkeep or repair necessary!

Curbs & Gutters

A concrete curb and gutter combination also creates a solid border for pavers to lay the road surface in between.

Dumpster Pads & Loading Docks

These areas need an extra boost in strength to withstand the high traffic and high impact.

Stairs & Breezeways

Concrete should be your material of choice to ensure a strong, enduring walkway.

DRAINING AND UTILITIES
