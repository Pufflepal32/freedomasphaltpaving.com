Trusted Commercial Paving Company Serving North Carolina’s Cities & Communities

North Carolina’s climate brings unique challenges for property owners and managers. From the freeze-thaw cycles in the winter to the heat and humidity in the summer, pavement surfaces face year-round stress. 

Turner Asphalt is the paving company local businesses trust to keep their properties accessible, safe, and looking professional in every season.

With nearly 30 years of experience across the Carolinas, our in-house crews bring unmatched local expertise to every project. We specialize in asphalt paving, concrete paving, milling, repairs, and a full range of specialty solutions tailored to your property’s needs.

Municipalities & Utility Contractors

Full-Service Concrete & Asphalt Paving Company for Your North Carolina Property

When you choose Turner Asphalt, you’re choosing a partner that can handle every stage of your paving project. As a full-service paving contractor, we install asphalt, pour concrete, perform repairs, and complete specialty services with our own self-performing crews. 

Our extensive concrete and asphalt services include:

Parking Lot Paving

Your parking lot is often the first impression visitors have of your business. We install and maintain asphalt parking lots designed for durability and compliance. 

Asphalt Repair & Maintenance

Over time, traffic, weather, and age take a toll on your asphalt surface. Our asphalt repair services include pothole patching, sealcoating, crack filling, and other targeted solutions that extend the life of your surface. 

By addressing issues early, we help property owners avoid liability risks and costly disruptions caused by unsafe pavement.

Concrete Paving & Curbing

We install concrete features in North Carolina, including sidewalks, curbs, concrete pads, and more. Our concrete services enhance both function and appearance, providing business owners with durable surfaces that withstand heavy traffic and weather.

Other Specialty Paving Contractor Services in Atlanta

Along with our extensive concrete and asphalt paving services, we also offer additional services that improve the overall appearance and longevity of your pavement. These services include: 

  • Drainage & Utilities: We design drainage systems that manage stormwater and protect your pavement.
  • Safety Features: Our asphalt and concrete contractors install bollards, guardrails, wheel stops, and other protective features that improve traffic flow and safety.
  • Snow Plowing & Ice Management: We work to keep your property safe and accessible during winter conditions.
  • Soil Stabilization: Strengthening the soil beneath your pavement helps support new pavement structures.
  • Sports Courts & Recreational Surfaces: Our team builds and resurfaces basketball courts, pickleball courts, and more.
